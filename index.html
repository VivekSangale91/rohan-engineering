Who We Are

We are pleased to introduce Rohan Engineering, established in 2019, and recognized for our expertise in pipeline fabrication and industrial structure fabrication work. Over the years, we have built a reputation for delivering reliable, high-quality engineering solutions tailored to client needs.

Our services extend beyond fabrication. We provide material selection support in line with ASME and ASTM standards, ensuring compliance and durability. We are highly skilled in pipeline inspection and in calculating the remaining life of pipelines, helping clients plan for long-term operational efficiency. Our team also specializes in corrosion under insulation (CUI) studies, offering valuable insights to enhance pipeline and structural reliability.

With a strong team of engineers, we excel in technical analysis, project scheduling, and execution planning. In addition, we provide insulation services and supply high-quality materials as per customer requirements. Our operations strictly follow good welding practices and procedures, ensuring safety, precision, and longevity in every project.
